Frequently Asked Questions

Is the donation process safe?

All donations are handled with Stripe! A large proportion of payments made over the internet are made possible by Stripe. With our implementation, your card details and sensitive information never touch our server. They are handled and processed securely with world-class encryption by Stripe. You can rest assured you're in safe hands. All payments are also refundable, just send us an email!

What percentage of my donation will be received by MRMJC?

Stripe charge a 2.2% fee + 30 cents. Unfortunately, we cannot control this. Every cent outside of this fee will go directly to the cause. I [Jude Cornish] am paying for the hosting and domain of this site out of my own pocket and all other aspects are covered by the founders.

Can I refund a donation?

All donations can be refunded. Just send us an email! You can also refund donations made on a recurring basis, as well as the subscription itself.

Can I cancel a recurring donation?

Of course! We understand situations may change. Just send us an email and we can refund you for any amount up to when the subscription was started. As well as cancel the recurring payment altogether.

Can I donate in a different currency?

You can donate in any currency but the amount that you enter should be in USD. We are looking to incorporate an option for different currency inputs soon. Apologies for any inconvinience caused, you can use the handy currency converter below if needed! Please note, the currency conversion rate you find may differ from the one charged by Stripe, but this difference should be negligible in almost all cases.
