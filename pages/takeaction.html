Reach out to a Karen Community near you

Thousands of Karen people have been resettled to third countries all over the world, including the United States, Canada, Australia, England, Norway, and Japan. Resettlement is an arduous process for refugees. Even as they flee persecution and violence from their homes, they never truly escape. Loss of loved ones, homes, their sense of security, safety, and dignity can follow refugees wherever they end up.

Research to see if there’s a Karen community near you and if there are any organizations that you can volunteer at to help out!
