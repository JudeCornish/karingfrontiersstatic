Donate to Karing Frontiers

Join Karing Frontiers in the mission of securing the right of education among refugee and migrant youth on the Thai-Myanmar border! This year, Karing Frontiers is aiming to raise 302,000 HKD to empower 534 students and 60 teaching staff among five different post-secondary schools located in refugee camps. $560 HKD is all it takes to sponsor one student for a whole academic year.

For every ticket you purchase, your name will be entered into the raffle, where you will have the chance to win prizes which include artwork exhibited at the Hong Kong Human Rights Art Prize, an $800 HKD gift voucher at the feel good factor beauty spa, portraits of the Chin and Kayan people by award winning photographer Jo Farrell, and more.

To enter the raffle, make a donation of at least $560 HKD. But feel free to also donate any amount that you can! Thank you for your kindness!
